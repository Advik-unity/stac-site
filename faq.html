FAQs What is the relationship to OGC’s WFS 3 specification? STAC intends to be fully compliant with the upcoming WFS 3 specification including implementing WFS 3’s query methods I have geospatial raster data, should I use STAC? Yes! So long as you can address your data in space and time and link directly to your data. I have vector data, should I use STAC? No. Vector data should be handled directly with WFS3. I have non-geospatial data, should I use STAC? No. Data that has no context in space and time is not suited to STAC. How do I publish additional data?
